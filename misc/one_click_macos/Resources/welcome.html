AlphaMap

TODO CHECK TEXT AlphaMap enables the exploration of proteomic datasets on the peptide level. It is possible to evaluate the sequence coverage of any identified protein and its post-translational modifications (PTMs). AlphaMap further integrates all available UniProt sequence annotations as well as information about proteolytic cleavage sites.

TODO CHECK TEXT AlphaMap was developed by the Mann Labs at the Max Planck Institute of Biochemistry and is freely available with an Apache License.
